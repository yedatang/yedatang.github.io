  1. Summary

   It can be seen from these tables that for both platforms, the most popular show genre is talent show, since it has the highest view counts and media rate. However, audiences give the lowest scores for these shows, which may due to the controversial results in talent competitions. On the other hand, audiences think highly of travel shows, but this genre is of low awareness from the perspective of view counts and media rate. One genre that performs well in both fields is the dating/relationship show.

   When comparing the two platforms, we can see that Tencent Video has better performance generally, especially in view counts and audience rate. This may partially due to their advantage in star influence, since they have the 4 out of the 5 most influential stars. Still, IQiyi's programs are well-known since they have high media rate.
