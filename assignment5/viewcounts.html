View Counts

   This page tells you about the view counts of each show. Lauched period refers to the total number of days since the show's release date. Accelerated view counts refers to the total amount of view counts up to the date of 10/26/2018. Average view counts = accerated view counts/lauched period, which becomes the average view count per day. Highest daily view counts refers to the highest single day view counts in the past month.

   You may change the records order by clicking the arrows beside the field title.
